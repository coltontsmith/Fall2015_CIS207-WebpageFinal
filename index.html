Welcome to Cheaha PC repair!

Cheaha PC Repair is your one stop shop for all of your technology related woes! We're a local business nestled in cozy Oxford, Alabama just north of the great Cheaha Mountain. We're looking forward to assisting you with your computer related problems. Whether you're running into trouble with malicious malware, experiencing slow web-browsing, having issues with mismatched drivers for your peripherals, or almost anything else that would cause your PC to misbehave, we've got you covered. We can operate on a wide range of devices and are very flexible to anything that you may need help with. Feel free to get in contact with us to schedule an appointment, or swing on by if you're in the area to browse some of our in-store merchandise. We have plenty of equipment and components to help out some of the local DIY'ers with their PC modding projects.
